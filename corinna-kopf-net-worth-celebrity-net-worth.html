    What is Corinna Kopf's net worth?

    Corinna Kopf is an American social media personality and content creator who has a net worth of $12 million. Corinna Kopf, AKA "Pouty Girl," is best known for her popularity on Instagram, YouTube, OnlyFans, Twitch, and Facebook Gaming. As we detail in the next section, it has been claimed that Corinna makes $1 million per MONTH from OnlyFans.

    OnlyFans Earnings

    In August of 2021, Corinna revealed that she earned roughly $4 million from OnlyFans in ONE MONTH. That report is unverified, but if it's half true, she is one of the platform's highest earning personalities. In a more recent average month, she has claimed to earn $1 million.

    As if that wasn't astonishing enough, in a later interview on Logan Paul's Impaulsive podcast, Corinna revealed that even months after joining OnlyFans she was still earning over $1 million every month from the adult platform. Kopf has over 1.56 million likes on OnlyFans.

    Early Life

    Corinna Kopf was born on December 1, 1995, in Palatine, Illinois, to a family of German descent. Most of her family is settled in Missouri, Iowa, and Wisconsin, and she has a sister named Sophia. Corinna attended a private school in Malibu for her early education. While still a student, she worked as a nanny for extra money and financial independence. She graduated high school but later decided to drop out of college to pursue a full-time social media career.

    (Photo by David Becker/Getty Images)

    Kopf started her Instagram in 2012 in which she posted pictures and videos of herself, and she now has more than 6.9 million followers. One of her most famous posts on the platform is a picture of herself attending the 2014 Spring Awakening music festival. She then created a Twitter account and quickly garnered hundreds of thousands of followers.

    Corina started a YouTube channel after conducting a poll on Twitter asking her fans if they'd like to see her there, and she began posting storytime videos. But her first YouTube appearance was on a prank channel called "Second Class." And her popularity sky-rocketed when she joined YouTuber David Dobrik as one of the members of the "Vlog Squad." She has also collaborated with Liza Koshy.

    In 2021, Corinna went solo posting more content on her YouTube channel and Instagram handle covering topics of lifestyle, beauty, and fashion. One of her most popular videos is one in which she describes her lip injection experience gone wrong. Corrina Kopf is also a Fortnite gamer who has a streaming deal with Facebook Gaming after streaming on Twitch. She has over 1 million followers on each of those platforms.

    Personal Life

    Corinna Kopf has dated Turner "Tfue" Tenney and Toddy Smith, both of whom are also fellow content creators. In December of 2022, it was revealed on Instagram that she was dating Sammy Wilk.

    Fans love her for her down-to-earth and honest personality, and she is a big mental health advocate after suffering from anxiety for most of her life.

    She grew up loving animals and was raised in a house with several dogs, and she originally planned on becoming a veterinary physician.

    Corrina Kopf loves piercings and has over 12 tattoos. She currently lives in Los Angeles, California.
