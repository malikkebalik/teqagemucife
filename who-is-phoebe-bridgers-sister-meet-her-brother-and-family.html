    Everyone is curious to know about Phoebe Bridgers Sister. Phoebe has been nominated for four Grammy Awards, including Best New Artist.

    She was a singer with Sloppy Jane before going solo, and her other talents include playing the guitar and producing records.

    When it was released in 2017, Stranger in the Alps by Bridgers garnered favorable reviews. Her second album, Punisher, was released in 2020 and earned many good reviews.

    At the 63rd Annual Grammy prizes, Bridgers was nominated for four prizes, including Best New Artist.

    In addition to performing as a solo artist, Bridgers also collaborates with Lucy Dacus and Julien Baker in the musical groups Better Oblivion Community Center and Boygenius.

    Does Phoebe Bridgers Have A Sister?

    Phoebe Bridgers doesn’t have a sister but grew up with her brother, Jackson Bridgers. 

    Phoebe and Jackson Bridgers, her younger brother, have the same parents. When Jackson was still a child and Phoebe was around 19, their parents separated.

    On August 17, 1994, Phoebe Lucille Bridgers was born in Pasadena, California. While her Father was a set for film and television, Jamie, her mother, works in real estate and stand-up comedy.

    Although she was born and reared in Pasadena, she also had some of her early years in Ukiah. She began playing the guitar at 13, and as a young girl, she busked at the Pasadena Farmers Market to earn additional money.

    After graduating from the Sequoyah School, Phoebe studied vocal jazz at the Los Angeles County High School for the Arts in 2009.

    Who are Phoebe Bridgers parents? Family Details

    Jamie Bridgers, who held several jobs, including Executive assistant and receptionist, reared Bridgers and her brother Jackson.

    Bridgers’ connection with her Father was tense. In a GQ article from September 2019, she said that her parents divorced when she was 20. She alleged that her Father drugged and beat her as a child.

    The 2020 song Kyoto by Bridgers was about the author’s Father and how she felt about him. She also expressed uncertainty about her capacity to pardon him for his prior actions.

    She mentioned in an NPR interview that her Father is oddly both emotionally present and absent.

    Is Phoebe Bridgers Married?

    Bridgers started dating American musician Ryan Adams in 2014. She was about 20, and he was about 40 at the time. At an unknown point, they broke up.

    Her song “Motion Sickness” is about their love. Bridgers and six other women accused Adams of verbal and emotional abuse in a 2019 piece by The New York Times.

    Later, until 2017, Bridgers was romantically linked to American musician Marshall Vore, her touring drummer and a collaborator on other tracks.

    They remain close and work together frequently; they also co-wrote the famous song “ICU” by Bridgers, based on their romance. At the time, Bridgers was seeing Irish Actor Paul Mescal.

    Phoebe Bridgers Sexuality

    After coming out as bisexual, Bridgers and her mother eventually made amends, and her mother later became an LGBT rights activist. Bridgers acknowledges that her classmates would make fun of her for eating fish as a teenager.

    Bridgers has spoken about how she has sought therapy for her struggles with anxiety and hopelessness.

    Phoebe admitted to having an abortion in October 2021. She claimed everyone should be able to do so after the Supreme Court’s draft decision overturning Roe v. Wade was published in May 2022.
