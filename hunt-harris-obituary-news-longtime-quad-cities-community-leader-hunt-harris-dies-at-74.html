    Hunt Harris Obituary – Cause of Death: Longtime Quad Cities community leader, Hunt Harris dies at 74.

    John Huntington “Hunt” Harris II of Naperville, formerly of Moline, passed away at the age of 74 on Wednesday, December 20 in Naperville after electing to begin hospice after multiple medical complications, his obituary said.

    Hunt as Quad Cities community leader supported multiple Quad City initiatives and non-profit organizations.

    Hunt was born on June 13,1949 to John Huntington Harris and Lucille Hanson Harris. He attended Moline public schools where he graduated co-valedictorian of his 1967 class. In 2005 he was inducted into the Moline High School Hall of Fame. He graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Pomona College with a degree in Economics and won the Pendleton Prize in Economics in 1971. He graduated from the Stanford University Graduate School of Business with a master’s degree from the Sloan Program in 1979.

    A Memorial Service will be held in Moline at Trimble Funeral Home on Saturday, January 13th, 2024, at 12pm. A celebration of life will follow from 1-4pm.
