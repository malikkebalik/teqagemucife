    Westwood Management Corporation is a large advisory firm based in Chicago. It manages $671.71 million of regulatory assets for 112 client accounts. It has been registered with the SEC as an adviser since 2001 and has operated in the jurisdictions of California, Florida, and Illinois.

    Westwood Management Corporation provides portfolio management for individuals and small businesses and portfolio management for businesses and institutional clients. It doesn't provide financial planning services to its clients. On top of advisory services, the firm doesn't engage in other business activities.

    Westwood Management Corporation: assets under management

    The assets under management (AUM) of Westwood Management Corporation is $671.71 million, all of which is managed in discretionary accounts. The firm's AUM has grown by 12% since the beginning of its operation, and the company doesn't manage any assets belonging to foreign clients.


    The firm manages $671.71 million in discretionary accounts. On average, the discretionary accounts where all decisions are made on behalf of the client hold $6 million each.

    Investments

    The firm invests in non-exchange traded securities the most. They account for 96% of the total AUM. The second-largest asset type is cash and cash equivalents, which make up 2% of the firm’s portfolio. Investments in state and local bonds form 1% of the total invested assets.

    Fees & Commissions

    The advisory firm has several streams of revenue. Clients pay Westwood Management Corporation for their services and products based on a percentage of assets under management and fixed fees. Flat-fee based compensation is the most transparent and the one you can safely predict in advance. These fees don’t include brokerage commissions, interest, taxes, and other account expenses.
