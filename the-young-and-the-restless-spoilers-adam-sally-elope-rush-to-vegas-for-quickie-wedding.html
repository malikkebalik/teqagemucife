    The Young and the Restless (Y&R) spoilers tease that Adam Newman (Mark Grossman) and Sally Spectra (Courtney Hope) may elope in Vegas. Will these exes reunite with a quickie wedding?

    Adam recently had a little too much to drink and blurted out a marriage proposal to Sally. However, that liquid courage only emboldened Adam to speak from the heart.

    Maybe it wasn’t the best decision to propose in that tipsy state, but Adam meant it when he said he wanted to marry Sally.

    Adam will continue to stand by that marriage proposal, so Sally can agree to wed anytime she’s on the same page.

    Of course, Adam can see past the cold exterior Sally puts up. Sally is trying hard not to let her real feelings bleed through, but Adam knows her too well for her to hide them.

    After Sally confesses her deep connection to Adam and surrenders to some passionate moments behind closed doors, things will get even more complicated.

    Sally may start to accept that she can’t be satisfied by the boring safety that Nick Newman (Joshua Morrow) has to offer.

    It’s clear that Sally is someone who craves excitement and unpredictability.

    Although Sally has been pushing for something more stable and likes the idea of a drama-free romance, that just doesn’t fit who she is as a person.

    Sally is wild at heart, which is what makes wild Adam such a great match for her.

    It may take some time before Sally fully accepts that, but what happens once she inevitably does?

    Adam might propose once more and do it up right with an engagement ring this time.

    Then again, perhaps Sally could turn the tables and pop the question to Adam.

    Regardless, Adam definitely wants to marry Sally if the opportunity presents itself.

    It’s not like Sally and Adam would have very many supporters for a guest list anyway, so they might as well just elope.

    Las Vegas was a home away from home for Adam once upon a time.

    Adam lived there when he had amnesia and seemed to feel comfortable in that world he once navigated as “Spider.”

    It’s easy to imagine Adam and Sally catching a flight to Vegas for an elopement.

    Sally has been shoving down her impulsive side for far too long, so she may finally unleash it and do something crazy like marry Adam in a quickie wedding ceremony!

    The Young and the Restless spoilers say Sally will find it harder and harder to resist Adam, so maybe she should just cave in for good and tie the knot!
