    What is MC Chris' Net Worth?

    MC Chris is an American nerdcore rapper, voice actor, writer, and comedian who has a net worth of $600 thousand. MC Chris was born in Libertyville, Illinois in September 1975.

    His debut studio album Life's a Bitch and I'm Her Pimp was released in 2001 and he went on to release the albums Knowing is Half the Hassle in 2003, Eating's Not Cheating in 2004, Dungeon Master of Ceremonies in 2006, MC Chris Is Dead in 2008, MC Chris Goes To Hell in 2010, Marshmellow Playground in 2011, Race Wars in 2011, Foreverrr in 2014, MC Chris Is Dreaming in 2016, and Marshmellow Campground in 2017. He has appeared in or voiced roles for the TV series Sealab 2021, Welcome to Eltingville, The Brak Show, Aqua Teen Hunger Force, Space Ghost Coast to Coast, Fat Guy Stuck in Internet, and the movies Aqua Teen Hunger Force Colon Movie Film for Theaters and Nerdcore for Life.
