    What is Crystal Gayle's Net Worth?

Born Brenda Gail Webb on January 9, 1951 in Paintsville, Kentucky,she was raised in Wabash, Indiana, U.S. as her parents moved there, at the age of four. She is the youngest of eight children of a very poor family. Loretta Lynn's sister, she was inspired by Lynn to learn how to play the guitar and perform backup for her brother´s band. Upon graduating from High School, Brenda signed for Decca records in 1969. Decca Records already had a recording artist with the name Brenda, so her sister suggested she change it to Crystal, and so Crystal Gayle became her stage name.

After not wanting to meet Decca´s imposition of style, Gayle resigned and joined United Artists. Even though her musical style is country, Gayle´s most successful hit was a jazz song titled: "Don´t It Make My Brown Eyes Blue" as it topped the U.S. chart for four weeks.
