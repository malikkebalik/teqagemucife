    Age, Biography and Wiki

    Paul Bates is an American actor best known for his roles in the films The Waterboy (1998), The Nutty Professor (1996), and The Nutty Professor II: The Klumps (2000). He was born on December 24, 1958 in the United States. Bates began his career in the early 1980s, appearing in small roles in television shows such as The Jeffersons and The Facts of Life. He made his feature film debut in the 1988 comedy Coming to America, playing the role of a security guard. In the 1990s, Bates had a recurring role on the sitcom Martin, playing the character of "Tiny". He also appeared in the films The Nutty Professor (1996) and The Waterboy (1998). In 2000, he reprised his role as "Tiny" in the sequel The Nutty Professor II: The Klumps. Bates has also appeared in numerous television commercials, including ones for Burger King, Pepsi, and McDonald's. As of 2021, Paul Bates's net worth is estimated to be roughly $2 million.
