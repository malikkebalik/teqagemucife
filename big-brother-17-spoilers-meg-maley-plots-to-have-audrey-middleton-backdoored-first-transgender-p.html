    Big Brother 17 spoilers are teasing that Audrey Middleton is enemy number one in the BB17 house, and Meg Maley has devised a plot to evict her. If you have been following the Big Brother spoilers on the live feeds, then you are well aware that Audrey Middleton is stirring up a ton of drama in the house, and it has nothing to do with the fact that she is the first transgender player to be cast in the CBS reality TV show.

    According to the BB17 spoilers, Audrey basically has pulled a Devin (you remember the Season 16 train wreck, right?) and went into the game playing too hard. Anyone who is familiar with Big Brother knows that the first few weeks you just have to sit back and let people seal their own fate. Sadly, Audrey chose not to sit back, and opted to be one of the people who seal their own fate. It didn’t take long for some of the other houseguests to put two and two together and realize that Audrey is basically playing everyone and has more alliances than we can count on both hands.

    BB17 spoilers from the live feeds tease that Meg Maley is onto Audrey’s game and she has devised a plan to have her backdoored and evicted. Last night on the live feeds, Meg shared her plan with some members of the mega alliance. Meg thinks that it is too early to get their hands dirty, so they should let one of the other players win the Head of Household, and then convince them to backdoor Audrey after the Power of Veto competition. The only factor they have to worry about is making sure Audrey’s right-hand-man Clay doesn’t win POV and save her, or choose not to use it so that they can’t put Audrey up.

    Meg’s plan seems legit, but this early in the game with this many factors and players still in the house – it could easily go awry. Do you think that Meg should just sit back and let someone else worry about Audrey. After all, there are a ton of other players to worry about right now. Or is Audrey a huge threat who needs to be eliminated quickly? Let us know what you think in the comments below!
