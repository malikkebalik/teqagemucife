    What is Kiara Hunter's Net Worth?

    Kiara Hunter is a Canadian actress, writer, model, and singer who has a net worth of $500 thousand dollars. Born in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, Kiara Hunter was a professional cheerleader and a member of the Canadian National Swim Team. She went on to win multiple fitness-related pageants, including, Miss Fitness BC, Miss Fitness Canada (Silver Medalist), Miss Venus International, Miss Ujena of Canada, and Miss Fitness Body Pro of Ujena. She is also a former Miss Canada.

    She began her acting career in 1990, and has appeared in a steady string of guest starring and co-starring roles in television and film projects, including "The X-Files", "Bordello of Blood", "Viper", "Stargate SG-1", and "Alien Incursion". Her album, "We Can Play" was moderately successful, and her single, "Believe", reached #9 on the European dance charts. She also launched her own comic book series, entitled "HunterGirl", which is a combination of the story of Joan of Arc and "Bladerunner".
