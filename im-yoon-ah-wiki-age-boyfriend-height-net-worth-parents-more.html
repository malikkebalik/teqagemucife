    Meet Im Yoon Ah, Who Played Go Mi Ho in Big Mouth

    (임윤아) Im Yoon Ah (born on May 30, 1990) is a 32-years-old South Korean actress and model who is known for her impressive work in the television industry. She entered the acting business at the age of 27 with a show named “9 Ends 2 Out,” which aired in 2007.

    Her performance alongside Ji Chang-Wook in “The K2” was also awe-inspiring. Aside from acting, Yoona is also the sub-vocalist and lead dancer of the girl group Girls’ Generation.

    In 2022, Yoona gained immense fame after landing the role of Go Mi Ho in an action crime drama, “Big Mouth.” In this Korean show, Choong Hwan Oh filmed Yoon alongside artists like Lee Jong-Suk, Yoo Su-bin, Kim Do-Wan, and Yang Kyung-won.

    Do you know that Im Yoon auditioned more than 200 times to become part of Girls’ Generation before her final selection in 2002?

    How Old is Im Yoon Ah?

    On May 30, 1990, Im Yoon Ah | 임윤아 (age 32 years) took birth in Daerim-dong, Seoul, South Korea. She is the second daughter of her parents as she has an elder sister named Im Yoon-Jeong. As a child, Yoona dreamed of becoming a singer while admiring the girl group SES, Fin.

    Yoona was cast through SM Entertainment’s Saturday public audition in May 2002 when she was a sixth grader at Shinyoung Elementary School, and on that day, she sang Wax’s “Please” as an audition song and performed “Oops I did it again” by Britney Spears. It is said that she danced too.

    Subsequently, after spending five years as a trainee, she debuted with her Girls’ Generation.
    On the one hand, she focused on her music career; on the other hand, she paid due attention to her school studies.

    After graduating from Yeongnam Middle School in Yeongdeungpo – gu, she entered Daeyoung High School and graduated on February 11, 2009. At her graduation ceremony, Yoona was awarded the Arts and Physical Education Award, given to a student who has honored the school with her unique talent. After school, she joined Dongguk University, where she graduated with a bachelor’s degree in theater.

    The Acting & Music Career of Im Yoon Ah

    Despite being chosen for Girls’ Generation in 2002, Yoona made her formal debut in 2007 with the song “Reunited World.” The trio immediately gained notoriety as a popular national girl group due to the success of the songs “Tell Me Your Wish” and “Gee,” which were released in 2009.

    Yoona has performed with Girls’ Generation and in many TV dramas. She earned recognition through her drama appearances, which took place at the same time as her Girls’ Generation debut, by grabbing the public’s attention at the beginning of her music career. As Joo Young Shin, a third-year high school student, she made her acting debut in the love drama “9 Ends 2 Out,” which aired in July 2007.

    Gradually her popularity increased, and she landed more opportunities to work as an actress. In 2008 she starred in “You Are My Destiny” as Jang Sae Byuk, which helped her establish herself as a beloved TV personality. In the coming years, she did a few more series like “Cinderella Man,” “Love Rain,” “The Prime Minister and I,” “God of War Zhao Yun,” and “The K2.” She also appeared in a few movies starting with an action-comedy film titled “Exit,” which came in 2019.
